Biography

ZHENG HE is currently a Ph.D. candidate at Peking University Health Science Center (PKUHSC). He obtained a Master's degree from South China University of Technology (SCUT) in 2025 and a Bachelor's degree from China Medical University (CMU) in 2022.

His research focuses on understanding the complex relationships between body composition and cardiovascular health, utilizing methods such as Mendelian randomization to explore causal associations in diseases like familial hypercholesterolemia and hypertension.
